Content Creator Intern

Creative Remote Role

Project Overview

We are looking for a creative and enthusiastic Content Creator Intern to help produce engaging and high-quality content for various digital platforms. If you have a knack for storytelling and a passion for visual communication, this remote role is for you!

Your Role & Responsibilities:

  • Develop and write compelling blog posts, articles, and website copy.
  • Create engaging social media content (text, images, short videos).
  • Assist in brainstorming and developing content calendars.
  • Collaborate with teams to ensure content aligns with brand voice and objectives.
  • Proofread and edit content for clarity, grammar, and style.

Qualifications:

  • Excellent writing, editing, and proofreading skills in English.
  • Creativity and a keen eye for detail.
  • Familiarity with content creation for various social media platforms.
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines in a remote setup.
  • Bonus: Experience with graphic design tools (Canva, Adobe Suite).

Why This Opportunity?

  • Opportunity to showcase your creative talents and build a diverse portfolio.
  • Work on a variety of content types for different projects.
  • Receive guidance and mentorship from experienced content professionals.
  • Flexible remote work environment.

Quick Facts

Role Type: Internship
Duration: 3 Months (Extendable)
Location: Remote
Stipend: Project-based / To be discussed
